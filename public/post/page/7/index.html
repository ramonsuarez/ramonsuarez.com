Lettre à Jean Galler en faveur de la praline Noir 85

The Artist: lessons for entrepreneurs (don&#8217;t do as The Muppets do)

You never know who’s gonna be who or where you are going to end Don’t treat those that are not yet somebody with disrespect. Even when I worked in public affairs this was a must. You never know where people are going to be at next. You cannot know everything about everybody, specially of those that you just met or haven’t had the time to get acquainted yet. Today’s trainee, junior employee or young entrepreneur could be tomorrow’s big thing, not in the long time.

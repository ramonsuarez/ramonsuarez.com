A Mission Statement For a Company of One

One of the things that I have to improve to get my projects off the ground is project management. Among the books I’ve been reading, one has really hit home: Radical Focus: Achieving Your Most Important Goals with Objectives and Key Results. The title is self explanatory. To work with your OKR (Objectives and Key Results) they recommend having a mission statement for the company to guide you. My experience with the Mission, Vision and Values of Betacowork was very positive and helpful.

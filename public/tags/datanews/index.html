Top 50 Tech Twitter Influencers in Belgium

This year they have moved away from Klout to rank the influencers and have started using the ranking made by the Belgian startup Tribalytics, created by the betacoworker Mehdi El Fadil who has been recently joined by Aleyda Solis.
